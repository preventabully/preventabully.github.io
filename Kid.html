TALE OF COOLSVILLE
Whats up kid, welcome to Coolsville!

You're probably wondering what's up with this squishy dude.

His name is Blobby, he won't hurt you. His job is to actually keep you company and to spread love!

He wouldn't harm anyone or anything! Not even a little old worm...
Actually, his best friend is a worm!

His name is Wrigglez and people around the streets call him a cool guy.

Head always held high, and his shoes always lookin' fly.

He calls himself the big boss around here but don't tell him I said this... But King Slowpe is ruler of all.
In this kingdom, King Slowpe is the chief in justice.

He has his mighty Glove of Dicipline to make anyone he touches behave immediately.

That's some power.

Wrigglez seems a little bit jealous with King Slowpe's fame and glory...

Don't tell him I said that.

Why is he jealous? Well that's because King Slowpe creates peace in Coolsville.

Pretty soon the village will be filled with happiness once more.
Yep, I said once more. This evil ghost named Lameo has haunted Coolsville for quite sometime.

He becomes more powerful the more people he pushes or makes fun of.

King Slowpe has not found him yet. People report the incidents too late.

Making him unable to be caught.

One evening, Blobby and Wrigglez were on their afternoon band session.

Wrigglez plays the harmonica, and Blobby rocks out on his drums.

Being the worm he is, Wrigglez goes to fetch some dirt for his snack while Blobby continues jamming.

Suddenly, Blobby feels a chill down his spine.
Blobby suddenly lands on the floor, breaking his drumsticks.

"HEY FAT DUDE, YOU PLAY THE DRUMS? THAT'S PRETTY FUNNY CONSIDERING YOU HAVE NO ARMS."

His voice made Blobby shiver and cry.

"HAHA YOU SOUND LIKE A FART WHEN YOU CRY!"

Wrigglez came back and stood outside listening.

His little worm heart couldn't take all the insults that Lameo was throwing at Blobby.

He could go run to King Slowpe and end his bullying once and for all with the Glove of Dicipline...

But his pride and jealousy wouldn't let him.

"G-GET AWAY FROM B-BLOBBY YOU B-BULLY"

Wrigglez's voice sounded higher yet strangely lower than it usually is.

"HEY LIL' WORMY HOWS THE DIRT I POOPED OUT YESTERDAY HAHA"

"YOU WON'T GET AWAY WITH THIS!"

Ouch... That's gotta hurt.

I don't want to display violence in this website but it was pretty bad.

Wrigglez got a black eye and was cut completely in half!

Good thing worms regenerate.

"uhh you didnt come to me when he showed up..?"

"NO I THOUGHT I COULD TAKE HIM ON MY OWN IT'S NOT MY FAULT."

"wrigglez... you dont have to put your life in danger dude. im here for a reason. and that reason is to make sure you and my friends are safe. :)"

Wrigglez just got a whole new respect of King Slowpe. :)

Another couple of days past by...

Everything seems pretty simple in Coolsville.

Band practice is going as usual as it is.

And Wrigglez goes to get dirt once again.

"YOU ARE JUST SO EASY TO TARGET BLOB HAHA"

The evil laugh suddenly spooked Wrigglez a block away.

Wrigglez stood there, contemplating about what to do, considering revenge.

But unlike before, he starts wriggling as fast as he can to the castle.

"AND YOU'RE UGLY AND YOU-"

"i finally caught up to you lameo. youre pretty annoying to catch."

"NOOO DON'T TOUCH ME WITH THAT THING!"

King Slowpe touches Lameo with the Glove of Dicipline.

"NOOOOOOOOOO"

And poof.

Lameo turns into Something that looks like Blobby!

Blobby smiles as he rolls over to Lameo and inspects him.

They just look at each other and smile.

Blobby, Wrigglez, and his new friend Lameo are now a inseparable trio.

They spread cheer throughout the village and Wrigglez is filled with joy.

And now thanks to Blobby, Wrigglez, and King Slowpe...

Peace is finally restored to Coolsville!

THE END.
